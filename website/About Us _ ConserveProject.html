About Us

Posted on April 25, 2015May 4, 2015 by lvo1988

Who are we?

We’re 5 students from Cal State Fullerton aiming to help California ConServe resources! Saving the planet one small step at a time.

What is ConServe?

ConServe is an app that will contribute to the resolution of water and energy conservation and waste reduction through a gaming system of goal settings and point accumulation. The gaming function of the app appeals to children and the younger demographic to get them interested in conservation and develop the right habits early on. The functionality of the app will appeal to the adult audience because it tracks cost savings and goal settings. By turning a serious issue into an exciting and rewarding activity, ConServe will allow people to contribute to water and energy conservation, while feeling accountable for their actions, in an enjoyable way!

Why ConServe?

Conservation and “going green” has become an increasingly popular and important global mindset. As the dangers of global warming and the alarmingly finite amount of resources are becoming more apparent, being environmentally conscious has become more significant than ever.  Our group has created a resource conservation system to improve the continuous challenge of waste management, targeting water and energy conservation, waste reduction, and recycling in California, through the use of a mobile gaming app called ConServe.
