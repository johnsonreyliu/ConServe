    We Are One

    As individuals, we're able to do our part in order to help conserve. Yes we can save a gallon of water here and there or recycle a can or two, but we have strength in numbers. A single can recycled can be vast among thousands of people recycling and likewise a gallon of water reserved by a large group means having enough for the future. We can make a difference.

    With California’s extreme drought resulting in insufficient water to serve all water-right holders, it is up to us to put all efforts into conserving not just water, but other resources as well such as electricity, paper, and oil to make a better California for our future generations.
